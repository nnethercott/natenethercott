Hei! I'm a fourth year engineering student at Queen's University. That's kind of vague. I study mathematics and mechanical engineering in a program called Applied Mechanics. Soon I'll have graduated and I guess by that time I'll no longer be in fourth year.


I love working on technical projects which rely on fundamental ideas in engineering and being able to convert them into tangible applications. I made this website as a place to share these sorts of projects as well as others.


A few projects I included come from my involvement on student design teams at Queen's. With these teams I've been able to work on problems ranging from building a formula-type vehicle, to controlling technology with your brain.


Eventually I'd like to work on something which goes into space or travels really fast. Also energy systems are pretty cool.
