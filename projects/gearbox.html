Gearbox.

2020


  • A two-stage gearbox is designed for a small vehicle in a third year Mech Eng design project using Siemens NX
  • –
  • A gear ratio of 2.0 is chosen to increase torque during a hill-climb event, and when inverted, to optimize performance in a top-speed event
  • –
  • Total print time for the housing, gears, and shafts comes in at 8 hours
  • –
  • The two-stage designs allows for the 15kg vehicle with an electric motor operating at 1.7Nm to reach a max velocity of 1.31 m/s
